# Luam - Brand Identity

## Name
**Luam** (pronounced: loo-ahm)

## Tagline
Buy and Sell Pre-loved Items

## Brand Colors (Current)
- Primary: Blue (#2563EB)
- Background: Gray (#F9FAFB)
- Text: Dark Gray (#111827)

## What is Luam?

Luam is a marketplace platform where people can buy and sell pre-loved items - fashion, electronics, home goods, and more. Like Vinted, but with multi-currency support (EUR, USD, VND) built from the ground up.

## Key Features

1. **Multi-Currency** - Seamlessly buy and sell in EUR, USD, or VND
2. **Fair Fees** - Only 5% platform fee, transparent pricing
3. **Secure Payments** - Powered by Stripe Connect
4. **Easy Listing** - Upload photos, set your price, done
5. **Direct Messaging** - Chat with buyers and sellers
6. **Trust & Safety** - Ratings, reviews, and secure transactions

## Target Audience

- Sellers: People looking to declutter and make money from items they no longer use
- Buyers: Bargain hunters, sustainable shoppers, people looking for unique items
- Geographic focus: France, Vietnam, USA (multi-currency support)

## Brand Voice

- Friendly and approachable
- Transparent about fees and processes
- Empowering (you're in control)
- Community-focused
- Sustainable and eco-conscious

## Competitive Positioning

**vs Vinted:**
- Multi-currency from day one
- Lower fees (5% vs Vinted's variable fees)
- Cleaner, more modern interface

**vs eBay:**
- Simpler, focused on pre-loved items
- No auctions, just fixed prices
- Better for fashion and everyday items

**vs Facebook Marketplace:**
- Safer payments (Stripe vs cash/Venmo)
- Better search and discovery
- Shipping built-in
